About Us

Come to Dookii’s wonderful pet grooming store Where your pets will be hair cut, groomed and washed for you at very affordable prices. Our staff is full of nice people who are good recommendations in this professional field. We are also willing to travel to your homes for this service. Enjoy your pets and our grooming care!

In 2025 Jonathan Stuppin was tired of all these dogs smelling bad. He made it his mission to keep doggies clean. After launching his business, he started selling pets out of the store front as well. These includes dogs and cats as well as pet birds and iguanas. The owners are now Samantha and Patrick O’brian.
